Electric Guitars 101

Amps

A guitar amplifier (or amp) is an electronic device or system that strengthens the electrical signal from a pickup on an electric guitar, bass guitar, or acoustic guitar so that it can produce sound through one or more loudspeakers, which are typically housed in a wooden cabinet.

  • Mesa Boogie are giants within the amp world. At the forefront of modern amplifier design, the company has established a reputation for its supreme build quality and game-changing tones.
  • Marshall is arguably one of the biggest names in electric guitar amplification. With their legendary amps adorning the stages of some of rock’s biggest names, their products remain a popular choice in the 21st Century.
  • Supro are one of *the* companies synonymous with rock music. Their guitars and amps have been weilded by The Rolling Stones, Led Zeppelin and David Bowie on some of their biggest ever records. A rich history indeed.
  • Orange Amps are regarded as one of the best amp companies around, sticking to their guns as a small amp manufacturer that does big things. And they're proudly British!
  • Vox encapsulate everything there is to love about those classic gritty British amp tones. Vox are one of worlds most well-known guitar amp manufacturers thanks to users like Brian May from Queen & The Beatles.
